1. Please use suitable fine, microfiber or gemstone polishing clothes to clean your jewellery.
2. Please Don't use any chemical, ultrasonic cleaners or any other cleaning agents for cleaning your jewellery.
3. We suggested for removing your jewellery before participating in any activities or adventure for avoiding the damage of your jewellery . Like: showering, swimming activities, gardening, dishes, fishing, forest picnic etc.
4. Refrain from exposing your jewelry to extreme temperature environments, hot or cold.
5. Refrain from storing your jewelry in extremely dry or extreme moisture environments for long periods of time.
6. These care tips will keep your jewelry look beautiful for a lifetime
